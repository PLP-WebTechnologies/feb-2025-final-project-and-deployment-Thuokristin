Overview

Theme Features

  1. Fully Responsive Design.
  2. Powerfull Admin Panel , Check it
  3. Well Documented
  4. Easy to Customize.
  5. Unlimited Colors
  6. 3 Layout Option : Wide , Boxed , Framed
  7. Support Custom Background
  8. Featured Posts Slider With Two Popreties (Custom Label, Recent Posts)
  9. Live Customizer : Colors, Fonts.

Getting Started

Upload Theme

Step 1 : You can install the theme by going to the following path: Dashboard > Theme > Backup / Restore > choose the "Korve.xml" file and click on Import from computer button and save , see images below & well done!



Step 2 : You can also install your theme by going to the following path: Dashboard > Theme > Theme HTML click on it and select all using keyboard shortcut Ctrl+A and delete all, then you will go to the "Korve.xml" file and open them with an editor software like "Notepad" and select all and come back again to blogger editor and paste all this code and save tempalte, see tutorial video to know how & well done!

Activate Responsive

You can Activate the responsive for mobile and tablet and other devices by going to the following path: Dashboard > Theme > Settings Button for mobile and click on it!

Now Select No. Show desktop template on mobile devices! and click on save! and well done!

Template Option's

Boxed Style

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Boxed Style &gr; click edit and type boxed for the Boxed Layout And type framed for framed Layout , and you well done! and if want to get back the wide layout just delete " boxed or framed" .

Hide Footer

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Hide Footer &gr; click edit and type hide and you well done! and if want to show the footer again just delete " hide " or type Show .

Dark Skin

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Dark Skin &gr; click edit and type dark and you well done! and if want to active again the light skin just delete " dark " or type light .

Hide Favorite Posts

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Hide Favorite Posts &gr; click edit and type hide and you well done! and if want to show the Favorite Posts again just delete " hide " or type Show .

Hide Instagram Widget

You can show and hide " Instagram Widget " just go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Hide Instagram Widget and click on Edit and type hide and click save and you well done!

For Example

Center Logo

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Center Logo &gr; click edit and type center that will hide the Ads and center the logo , and you well done! and if want to get back the Ads just delete " center " and click save .

Hide Sidebar From Home Page

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Hide Sidebar From Home Page &gr; click edit and type hide that will hide the " Sidebar " from the Home page only and you well done! and if want to show it again just delete " hide " or type Show .

Sidebar Post Number

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Layout > Theme Options > Sidebar Post Number &gr; click edit , Now you have 3 options show the slider post number you have " 1 , 2 , 3 " just type the number you want and you well done! .

Post Or Page Layouts

Go to your dashboard > Your Blog > Posts or Pages > Select one > Type this shortcode "[left-sidebar]" if you want to apply Left Sidebar Layout on your page or post, or type "[right-sidebar]" if you want to apply Right Sidebar Layout on your page or post, or type "[full-width]" if you want to apply Full Width Layout on your page or post & you well done! 

            
 
[left-sidebar] / [right-sidebar] / [full-width]
